Meet our team (from left to right): Astha, Mansi, Vaishali, and Atiya.

We are Computer Engineering students at Toronto Metropolitan University, and this Library Management System was developed as part of our final capstone project for 2025. Inspired by concepts from our third-year course, COE692: Software Design and Architecture, we applied our knowledge to design and implement a Python-based application using a microservices architecture.

üìö Project Purpose

Our project is a cloud-native, Python-based Library Management System (LMS) designed to enhance the efficiency, scalability, and usability of managing both physical and digital library resources. Built with a microservices architecture using FastAPI, MongoDB, Docker, and Kubernetes and deployed on Google Cloud, our system streamlines key features like book borrowing, reservations, user authentication, and personalized recommendations. Our goal was to modernize library operations while offering an intuitive, scalable, and user-friendly experience for both patrons and administrators.
